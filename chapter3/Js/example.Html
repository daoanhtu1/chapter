ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • 1tbs butter
  • 2tbs cream

method

  1. melt butter in a frying pan over a medium heat
  2. Gently mix the eggs and cream in a bowl
  3. Once butter has melted add cream in a bowl
  4. Using a spatula fold the eggs from the edge of the pan to the center every 20s(as if you are making an omlette)
  5. When the eggs are still moist remove from the heat(it will countinue to cook on the plate until seved)
